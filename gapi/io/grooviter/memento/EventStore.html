Package: io.grooviter.memento

[Groovy] Interface EventStore


  • interface EventStore

    Responsible for storing and retrieving events keeping and immutable stream of events. It follows some of the ideas contained at: https://martinfowler.com/eaaDev/EventSourcing.html

    Since:
    0.1.0
